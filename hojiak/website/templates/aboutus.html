Hojiak Cheerleader

As a foodie and tech enthusiast, Suz wishes to contribute to the local food scene by bringing it even closer to people through technology.

Suz refuels her cheerleading spirit by eating her favourite satay beehoon and black carrot cake. If she is not cheering the team on, you find her herding cats.

Hojiak Creative Pixie

Genevieve has been actively volunteering at community events, a hospital and non-profit organisations. She believes in doing a small part in making the community a little better.

Marketing, branding and a dash of sparkle is what she brings to the Hojiak table.

Hojiak Spiderman

Song Jian is a self proclaimed "Foodie KayPoh", believing in quality over appearance of food. Sometimes you can catch him interacting with the hawkers and chefs, mostly to unlock the secrets to making good food.

As Hojiak Spiderman, he is well connected and links the team up with relevant people to work with.

Hojiak Warrior

As the Hojiak warrior, Shun Yuan fights for the livelihood and culture of Singapore hawkers. She researches on the history and current evolution of hawkers, aiming to reinvent the notion of hawkerpreneurship for the future.

Her favourite hawker food is chicken rice (without chicken) because she loves the taste of the rice more than anything else.

Hojiak Tech Ninja

Kevin is a newbie tech enthusiast who is looking to transition into the tech industry.

As tech ninja, he helps to develop the Hojiak website. He enjoys a warm cup of kopi-c-kosong in his free time.
